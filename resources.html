Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I polish my marble floors?

For high-traffic areas, professional polishing every 12-18 months is recommended. Less frequented areas can go 2-3 years between polishing sessions.

What's the difference between crystallization and diamond polishing?

Crystallization uses chemical compounds to create a shine, while diamond polishing is a mechanical process using progressively finer diamond abrasives for a natural luster.

How long does marble polishing take?

The time varies depending on the area size, marble condition, and chosen method. Typically, a standard room (200-300 sq ft) takes about 4-6 hours to complete.
